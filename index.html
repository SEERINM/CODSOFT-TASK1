Traffic Diversion and Congestion Management

Brief Description About My Embedded IoT Based Project

The Traffic Diversion System for Road Congestion Management project leverages IoT technology to alleviate traffic congestion by dynamically redirecting vehicles. By integrating ultrasonic sensors, the Blynk platform, and real-time data analysis, the system monitors traffic flow and detects congestion. Ultrasonic sensors measure vehicle presence and distance, triggering notifications and buzzer alerts to indicate heavy traffic conditions. This information is transmitted to the Blynk web console, allowing remote monitoring and control. Overall, the Traffic Diversion System represents a significant advancement in traffic management, offering a scalable, efficient, and flexible solution to urban congestion challenges.
